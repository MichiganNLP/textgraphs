Paper Presentation Information

NEW! Authors of accepted papers will have to prepare only an oral presentation of the paper.

Long papers will have a 25 minutes presentation.
Short papers will have a 15 minutes presentation.
Demos will have a 10 minutes presentation.

Submission Information

NEW! Camera-ready submission guidelines. All submissions must be electronic in PDF and must be formatted using the EMNLP 2013 Style Files



Paper submission site is here: Submissions



Submissions will consist of:
  • Full papers of max. 8 pages (one additional page for the Reference section only is allowed - for a maximum of 9 pages)
  • Short papers of max. 4 pages (one additional page for the Reference section only is allowed - for a maximum of 5 pages)
  • Position papers of max. 4 pages(one additional page for the Reference section only is allowed - for a maximum of 5 pages)
  • Demos papers of max. 4 pages(one additional page for the Reference section only is allowed - for a maximum of 5 pages)
All submissions must be electronic in PDF and must be formatted using the EMNLP 2013 Style Files (see EMNLP 2013 camera-ready guidelines.)

Author Instructions

  • Multiple-submission policy
    Papers that have been or will be submitted to other meetings or publications must indicate this at submission time. Authors submitting multiple papers to TextGraphs-8 may not submit papers that overlap significantly (50%) with each other in content or results. Authors of papers accepted for presentation at Textgraphs-8 must notify the organizers immediately as to whether the paper will be presented. All accepted papers must be presented at the conference in order to appear in the proceedings.
  • Blind review policy
    In order to facilitate blind reviewing, the authors should omit their names and affiliations from the paper. Furthermore, self-references that reveal the author identity, e.g., "We previously showed (Smith, 1991)" must be avoided. Instead, use citations such as "Smith previously showed (Smith, 1991)". Papers that do not conform to these requirements will be rejected without review.

    Papers should be submitted via the START Conference Manager site: Submissions
